The World Wide Web

The story behind the World Wide Web

Some facts about the World Wide Web:

  • The WWW was invented in 1990 to a select group of scientists
  • The web was made public in 1991
  • WWW code and protocol became royalty in 1993
  • The web is constantly developing and had many versions and variations over the years

The importance of the World Wide Web

Reasons why the WWW was a breakthrough in technology:

  • It allowed the world to be connected in a way never heard of before
  • it provided educational, medical and government facilities to exchange information
  • It was one of the first steps to digitally connecting our world.
  • It allowed international businesses to operate much easier
