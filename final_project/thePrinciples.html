Principals of Visual Design

5 Different design Principals of Visual Design:

  1. Scales
  2. Visual Hierarchy
  3. Balance
  4. Contrast
  5. Gestalt Principals

Scales

Scale refers to the use of relevent size to signal importance and rank in composition. The reason behind this element is simple, if an important element is big, its more likely to be notices

Visual Hierarchy

Visual Hierarchy refers to designing Content with the layout where what is important is more visible than the content which is less important. Visual Hierarchy Controls the User Experience by making it easier to find what they want.

Balance

The principle of balance is satifying and Arrangment and proportion of design and content on webpage where there is equal distribution

Contrast

Contrast provide a noticeable differece in two images that make them unique and different. it provides us a noticeable difference that we recognise.

Gestalt Principles

Gestalt Principle is hoq human simplify images and organise images. This principle of contrast is often applied trough colors.
